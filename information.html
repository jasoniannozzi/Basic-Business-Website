Gift Certificates

Gift Certificates for The Bridge Café can be requested via e-mail at giftcerts@bridgecafe.com. Requests are typically processed in 1 – 2 business days. Once your request has been made and processed, it will take 5 – 7 business days to arrive by mail in the United States. Gift Certificates are a great idea for any birthday or anniversary as our restaurant has been a host to many over the years.
