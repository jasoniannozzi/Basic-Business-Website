The Restaurant

Nestled riverside under the Brooklyn Bridge, The Bridge Café exudes romance with its breathtaking NYC skyline views, gorgeous atmosphere and above all our classic American cuisine. As a Michelin-starred restaurant, The Bridge Café similarly boasts one of the most outstanding wine lists in the world. Chef Wolfgang Puck’s menu proudly complements the changing seasons, featuring a classic American menu of elegance and refinement.

The romantic atmosphere is enhanced by wonderful flower arrangements, live piano music, and seamless service, therefore creating a perfect setting for the countless engagements that take place. Our elegant dining room, serious dress code and Michelin starred menu has been delighting our local clientele and visitors from around the world since 1977.
